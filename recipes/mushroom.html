Tuscan Butter Mushrooms

Description

Vegetarian, low-carb and popping with flavour, this creamy mushroom recipe can be enjoyed as a standalone dish, or with kojnac/shirataki noodles or zucchini pasta.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 lb. baby bella mushrooms, washed
  • 1 c. cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3/4 c. heavy cream
  • 1/4 c. grated Parmesan
  • 3 c. spinach
  • Kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter.
  2. Add garlic and tomato paste and cook until fragrant, 1 minute.
  3. Add mushrooms and tomatoes and cook until mushrooms are tender and tomatoes are starting to burst, 5 minutes.
  4. Add heavy cream and Parmesan and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes, then bring to a simmer.
  5. Add spinach and cook until sauce is thickened and spinach is wilted, 5 minutes.
