Egg Muffins 3 Ways

Description

Quick, convenient to-go breakfast to grab before going out the door. Cook these low-carb egg muffins ahead of time, refrigerate, grab and go!

Ingredients

  • Base

    • 12 large eggs
    • 2 tbsp. finely chopped onion
    • salt & paprika to taste

  • Tomato Spinach Mozzarella

    • 1/4 c. fresh spinach, chopped
    • 8 grape/cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1/4 c. shredded mozzarella cheese

  • Bacon Cheddar

    • 1/4 c. cooked bacon, chopped
    • 1/4 c. shredded cheddar cheese

  • Garlic Mushroom Pepper

    • 1/4 c. sliced brown mushrooms
    • 1/4 c. bell pepper, diced
    • 1 tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
    • 1/3 tsp. minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F | 180°C. Lightly spray a 12-cup capacity muffin tin with nonstick oil spray
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and onion. Season with salt and paprika, to taste
  3. Add egg mixture halfway up into each tin of a greased muffin tin
  4. Divide the three topping combinations into 4 muffin cups each
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until set
  6. Let cool slightly, then serve OR store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days and reheat when ready to serve
